Questions

Everything you're wondering.

How do you calculate my offer?+

We combine recent comparable sales in your neighborhood, current market conditions, your home's condition, and the cost of any work needed. The estimate above is directional; a real analyst confirms the verified offer within 24 hours.

Are there any fees or commissions?+

None. No agent commissions, no listing fees, and we cover standard closing costs. The cash number we agree on is what you walk away with.

Do I need to make repairs first?+

No. We buy houses completely as-is — outdated, damaged, even with tenants. Don't fix or clean a thing; we handle all of it after closing.

How fast can I actually close?+

Because we pay all cash, there's no lender to wait on. Many sellers close in 7 days. If you need more time, you pick a later date that works for you.

Is the offer really no-obligation?+

Completely. Get your offer, take your time, and only move forward if it's right for you. There's zero pressure and nothing to sign upfront.
